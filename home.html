Non-deficit barrier language, linked adjustments, and ready-to-polish NCCD comments – all in one tabbed workspace.

Designed around NCCD evidence.

NCCD Support helps teachers describe what they already do – with structured prompts for barrier descriptions, adjustments, frequency, duration and responsible staff. It is designed to sit alongside existing school processes and templates.

  • non-deficit wording: barriers foreground curriculum, environment and teaching factors.
  • structured comments: adjustment text includes frequency, duration and responsibility fields.
  • no logins or storage: all information is held only in the current browser session.
